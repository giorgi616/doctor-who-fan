main character

"The Doctor" was the main alias used by a mysterious renegade Time Lord from Gallifrey who travelled through time and space with various companions in
their obsolete and "borrowed" Type 40 TARDIS. They were the universe's "greatest defender", having saved the cosmos thousands of times throughout
a long life, becoming a great legend across the universe.
